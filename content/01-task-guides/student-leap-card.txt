TASK GUIDE: Student Leap Card (public transport)
==============================================================

TASK:          Get a TFI Student Leap Card
CATEGORY:      transport
PRIORITY:      Week 1–2
WHO NEEDS IT:  Full-time students who use public transport (bus,
               Luas, DART, train) in Ireland.
WHY:           Pays HALF-PRICE fares across the TFI network and is
               the cheapest way to travel. Also doubles as a student
               travel ID.

ELIGIBILITY:
  - Full-time education, 15+ hours per week
  - Course at least 6 months / 25 weeks long

STEPS:
  1. Apply online at leapcard.ie (student section /
     student.leapcard.ie).
  2. Upload supporting evidence: a letter from your college/
     institution confirming the course is full-time (15+ hrs) and
     over 6 months / 25 weeks. International students upload their
     university acceptance letter. Must be in English or Irish.
  3. Wait for the confirmation email saying your card is ready.
  4. Collect the card from a Student Leap Card agent — bring your
     student ID and valid proof of age.
  5. Top up with travel credit (online, app, shops, or machines).

DOCUMENTS:
  - University acceptance / enrolment letter (English or Irish)
  - Student ID (for collection)
  - Valid proof of age ID (for collection)

FEES:          €5 refundable deposit + minimum €5 travel credit
TIMEFRAME:     Apply online, wait for "ready" email, then collect.
               [VERIFY current processing time]

OTHER TRANSPORT (Dublin & nationwide):
  - Transport for Ireland (journey planner): https://www.transportforireland.ie
  - Dublin Bus: https://www.dublinbus.ie
  - Luas (Dublin tram, Red & Green lines): https://luas.ie
  - Irish Rail (DART & national trains): https://www.irishrail.ie
  - Bus Eireann (national bus network): https://www.buseireann.ie
  - Dublin Bikes (~€35/year cycle share): https://www.dublinbikes.ie
  - Taxi apps: Free Now https://www.free-now.com/ie/ | Uber https://www.uber.com/ie/en/
  - Driving a foreign licence? See driving-licence.txt.

OFFICIAL LINK:
  - https://student.leapcard.ie/  (or https://www.leapcard.ie)
  - https://www.transportforireland.ie/fares/leap-card/
  - TCD students: https://www.tcdsu.org/services/student-leap-card

TIPS:
  - Many Students' Unions (incl. TCD SU) are Leap Card agents — you
    can often apply and collect through them.
  - Set up auto top-up in the TFI Leap app so you never get caught
    short.
  - Tap on (and tap off on Luas/DART/rail) to be charged the correct
    fare.
