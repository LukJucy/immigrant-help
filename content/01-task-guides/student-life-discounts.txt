==============================================================
TASK GUIDE: Student Life & Discounts
==============================================================

TASK:          Get student discount cards & plug into student life
CATEGORY:      lifestyle
PRIORITY:      First month (nice-to-have, money-saving)
WHO NEEDS IT:  Any student wanting discounts and community.
WHY:           Student cards save money on travel, shopping, and
               attractions; student bodies offer support and events.

DISCOUNT CARDS:
  - ISIC (International Student Identity Card) - worldwide student
    discounts: https://www.isic.ie
  - Student Universal Card (discounts in Ireland):
    https://www.studentuniversalcard.ie
  - Your Student Leap Card also acts as student ID for many discounts
    (see student-leap-card.txt).

STUDENT BODIES:
  - Union of Students in Ireland (USI): https://usi.ie
  - Your college's own Students' Union (SU) - events, support, and
    often a Student Leap Card agent.

THINGS TO SEE & DO:
  - Discover Ireland: https://www.ireland.ie
  - Visit Dublin (official city guide): https://www.visitdublin.com

TIPS:
  - Attend Freshers' Week - join societies, meet people, grab
    freebies and discount sign-ups.
  - Always ask "is there a student discount?" - many cafes, cinemas,
    and shops offer one with a student ID.

LAST VERIFIED: 8 June 2026 (source: user pre-arrival guide)
