==============================================================
TASK GUIDE: PPSN (Personal Public Service Number)
==============================================================

TASK:          Get your PPSN
CATEGORY:      legal / money
PRIORITY:      First 90 days (do AFTER you have long-term accommodation)
WHO NEEDS IT:  All students who will work, pay tax, open some bank
               accounts, or access public/health services. Required
               before starting any paid job to avoid emergency tax.
WHY:           A unique ID number that links you to tax, social, and
               public services in Ireland. Without it you are taxed at
               the emergency rate and cannot be set up properly by an
               employer.

STEPS:
  1. Travel to Ireland first — you must be RESIDENT here to apply.
     You cannot get a PPSN from abroad.
  2. Secure long-term accommodation (student residence or private
     rented). Short-term/temporary accommodation is NOT accepted.
  3. Create a basic MyGovID account at mygovid.ie.
  4. Get a letter from your university/college stating you need a
     PPSN for your studies, addressed to the tax office. (See
     02-prefill-templates/letter-templates.txt.)
  5. Log in to MyWelfare.ie and apply for a PPSN online.
  6. Book the required IN-PERSON appointment as part of the online
     application. You will be told which documents to bring.
  7. Attend the appointment with your documents.
  8. Your PPSN is POSTED to your address afterwards (not given on
     the day).

DOCUMENTS:
  - Passport (photo ID)
  - Evidence of why you need a PPSN: the college PPSN letter
    (confirms it is needed for study)
  - Proof of address (long-term; the college letter is often
    accepted for this too)
  - MyGovID / MyWelfare account set up

FEES:          Free
TIMEFRAME:     Appointment booked within application; number arrives
               by post days–weeks later. [VERIFY current wait]

OFFICIAL LINK:
  - https://www.gov.ie/en/department-of-social-protection/services/get-a-personal-public-service-pps-number/
  - https://services.mywelfare.ie/en/topics/identity-services/personal-public-service-pps-number/
  - https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/

TIPS:
  - Sort accommodation FIRST — this is the most common blocker.
  - Ask your university's international/student office for the PPSN
    letter; many produce them as standard for new students.
  - You do NOT necessarily need a PPSN to use your university's own
    student health service.
  - Apply early — you need the PPSN before your first payslip to
    avoid emergency tax (see tax-revenue.txt).
