==============================================================
PREFILL: Letter & Email Templates
==============================================================

PURPOSE
-------
Draft letters/emails the site auto-fills from the user's profile.
{{placeholders}} are replaced with profile fields. Users review,
then send to their university office or use as supporting docs.

----------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE 1: Request to university for PPSN letter
(email FROM student TO international/student office)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Subject: Request for PPSN confirmation letter — {{full_name}}, {{student_id}}

Dear {{university}} International/Student Office,

I am a registered {{course_name}} student (student ID {{student_id}})
and I am applying for a PPSN (Personal Public Service Number) for the
purpose of my studies.

Could you please provide a letter, addressed to the tax office,
confirming that I am a full-time student and that I require a PPSN for
my studies? I understand this letter is accepted by the Department of
Social Protection as evidence for the PPSN application.

My details:
  Full name: {{full_name}}
  Date of birth: {{date_of_birth}}
  Nationality: {{nationality}}
  Irish address: {{irish_address}}
  Course: {{course_name}}
  Course start: {{course_start}}

Thank you very much for your help.

Kind regards,
{{full_name}}
{{phone_irish}} | {{email}}
