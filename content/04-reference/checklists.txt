==============================================================
REFERENCE: Stage Checklists (pre-arrival -> graduation)
==============================================================

PURPOSE
-------
Ready-made checklists the site renders as tick-lists, keyed to the
user's timeline. Source: user pre-arrival/after-arrival/visa guides.

----------------------------------------------------------------
3-6 MONTHS BEFORE ARRIVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Confirm your course is on the ILEP register
  [ ] Accept your college offer and pay tuition fees (keep proof)
  [ ] Arrange private health insurance (EUR 25,000+ cover)
  [ ] Ensure EUR 10,000 is accessible in your bank account
  [ ] Obtain your English language test certificate
  [ ] Apply for your student visa on AVATS (allow 4-12 weeks)
  [ ] Find accommodation in Ireland (start early!)

----------------------------------------------------------------
1-2 MONTHS BEFORE ARRIVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Confirm your visa has been approved
  [ ] Gather all ORIGINAL documents for IRP registration
  [ ] Book your flight (compare Ryanair, Aer Lingus)
  [ ] Confirm accommodation arrangements
  [ ] Open a Revolut/N26 account (works immediately, no PPSN)
  [ ] Get EHIC card if you are an EU student
  [ ] Download the Transport for Ireland app
  [ ] Note emergency numbers: 999 / 112
  [ ] Check if your driving licence needs conversion
  [ ] Contact your college's international student office

----------------------------------------------------------------
ON ARRIVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Keep passport and all documents accessible
  [ ] Note your arrival date (your 90-day IRP clock starts)
  [ ] Buy an Irish SIM card (airport/newsagent/supermarket)
  [ ] Book IRP appointment at inisonline.jahs.ie (same/next day!)

----------------------------------------------------------------
FIRST WEEK ON ARRIVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Book IRP appointment: inisonline.jahs.ie
  [ ] Register at your college and get your student card
  [ ] Apply for PPS number: mywelfare.ie
  [ ] Get a Student Leap card: leapcard.ie
  [ ] Buy an Irish SIM card (if not done at airport)
  [ ] Register with college health centre / a local GP
  [ ] Open a traditional bank account (AIB/BOI) for an Irish IBAN
  [ ] Attend Freshers' Week and join societies

----------------------------------------------------------------
WITHIN 90 DAYS OF ARRIVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Attend IRP registration appointment at Burgh Quay
  [ ] Pay EUR 300 registration fee
  [ ] Receive Stamp 2 - IRP card posted to your address

----------------------------------------------------------------
EACH YEAR
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Renew IRP online 12 weeks before expiry (not earlier)
  [ ] Renew health insurance
  [ ] Maintain enrolment and attendance at college

----------------------------------------------------------------
AFTER GRADUATION
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] Apply for Stamp 1G (graduate visa) before Stamp 2 expires
  [ ] Begin job search (you have 12-24 months)
