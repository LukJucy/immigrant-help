==============================================================
REFERENCE: Timeline & Deadlines (engine input)
==============================================================

PURPOSE
-------
Drives the personalised timeline view. Each task has a timing rule
relative to the user's arrival_date or course_start. The site sorts
tasks by computed due date and flags hard deadlines.

ANCHORS
  arrival_date  — when the student lands in Ireland
  course_start  — first day of term

PHASES
  PRE_ARRIVAL   — do before flying
  WEEK_1        — first days after landing
  FIRST_MONTH   — settle-in tasks
  FIRST_90_DAYS — legal must-dos
  WHEN_WORKING  — only if taking a job

----------------------------------------------------------------
TASK            PHASE          TIMING RULE                  HARD?
----------------------------------------------------------------
Student visa    PRE_ARRIVAL    apply 4-12 wks before        YES*
(visa-required   (AVATS)        course start; needs ILEP
 nationals)                     course + EUR 10k funds +
                               insurance + English cert
Accommodation   PRE_ARRIVAL    apply months before          no
                               (blocks PPSN + bank)
Health insurance PRE_ARRIVAL   before IRP appointment        yes*
SIM card        WEEK_1         day 1 (needed for portals)    no
Digital bank    PRE_ARRIVAL    optional, open before flying  no
                / WEEK_1
Book IRP appt   WEEK_1         book ASAP after arrival       no**
                               (waits 8-10 wks observed)
PPSN letter req WEEK_1         request from college early    no
PPSN apply      FIRST_MONTH    after long-term address       no
                               secured
Traditional bank WEEK_1-2      during orientation week       no
Student Leap     WEEK_1-2      after enrolment letter        no
GP registration  FIRST_MONTH   soon after arrival            no
IRP registration FIRST_90_DAYS arrival_date + 90 days        YES (legal)
Register job/tax WHEN_WORKING  before first payday           yes*
                               (avoid emergency tax)
IRP renewal     EACH_YEAR      from 12 wks before expiry,    YES
                               online, EUR 300 (don't let
                               card expire before travel)
Stamp 1G apply  AFTER_GRAD     before Stamp 2 expires        YES
                               (12-24 months to seek work)

  *  conditional/category hard deadline (visa needed before travel
     for visa-required nationals; insurance required for IRP; tax
     registration required before pay to avoid emergency tax)
  ** not itself a deadline, but the 8-10 week appointment wait means
     booking late risks missing the 90-day IRP deadline -> treat as
     urgent.

KEY COMPUTED DATES
  irp_deadline   = arrival_date + 90 days        (show countdown)
  book_irp_by    = irp_deadline - 70 days        (allow for wait)
  tax_register_by= first_work_day - 7 days

DISPLAY NOTES
  - Show the IRP 90-day countdown prominently on the dashboard.
  - Order the timeline by computed due date, grouped by phase.
  - Surface "blocked by" relationships: PPSN and bank both depend on
    a long-term address (accommodation).
